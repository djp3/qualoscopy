Qualoscopy

Colonoscopists are charged with preventing colorectal cancer. Yet, 7-9% of colorectal cancers occur within 5 years of colonoscopy. Interval cancer risk is reduced when the prep is excellent, cecum is visualized, withdrawal time is > 6 minutes, and when the colonoscopist has a high adenoma detection rate.

CMS is phasing in requirements to record and report these quality measures. Performance will be tied to reumbursement and will be publically available.

If you aren't ready, Qualoscopy is a good place to start.
